Revising the way we get news online

The Inflo Project is next-generation social media platform for media professionals and news enthusiasts, which leverages the power of the community to regulate content shared within the platform. Using the latest AI technologies to empower people to be in control of the content they see, and a social framework which rewards users for quality interaction, we intend to revise the way information is shared and accessed online, all made possible by a business model which rewards insights over engagement
