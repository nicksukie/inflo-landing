A revision in online news discussion

Inflo is a context hub for people who want to discuss the news constructively. It combines features of social media and news aggregators â€” with a few key differences: 1) a type-based commentary system for contributing one's input in a structured way, and an insight-based voting system to reward quality insights instead of polarized opinions; and 2) a non-algorithmic, ad-free feed which eliminates the factors that make clickbait and "fake" news lucrative. We don't think we know better than you about what you want to see.
