What is Inflo

Inflo is a project initiated in 2018 by in collaboration with the System and Media Lab (link) at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, as a revision on existing social media platforms for the purpose of sharing news. We have made use of cutting edge research and machine learning advances to create a new model for online content sharing which hold closely to the values of informative accuracy and reliability, without being exploited for profit through user data and ad targeting. The alpha platform was released in December 2019 with a limited subset of features. It can be accessed here.
